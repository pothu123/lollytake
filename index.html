About us

LollyTake is a video-on-demand Pay-Per-View platform owned by Bro and Brother Creations. Our adaptive and highly evolved video streaming technology ensures the best video quality based on the top-end bandwidth availability.

It's not only watching movies, you can win exciting cash prizes by answering simple questions at the end of each movie from the app itself.
